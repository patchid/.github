# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of Patch ID pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report violations to **conduct@patch-id.com**. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
